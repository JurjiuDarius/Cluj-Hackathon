1. Pet owner
- can log in
- can view all of his pet's data: age, weight, food specifications, medical history
- can upload documents for a pet
- can create an appointment
- can look up stuff he doesn't know in there animal's profile with AI
- can get summaries and explanations of documents with AI
- based on the animal's profile, get recommendations

2. Vet
- can create an account for a new patient
- can accept appointments 
- can leave recommendations for an appointment
- can modify an animal's history and profile

Nice to have:
- can track an animal's progress throughout a disease (owner and vet)

Backend: Flask, Postgres
Frontend: Angular s
